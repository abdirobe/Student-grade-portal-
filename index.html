Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Change Password" in your dashboard or contact Abdushekur Mohammed directly.

Where can I find my course materials?

Go to the "Course Materials" tab in your portal. All materials are organized by course and year.

How are final grades calculated?

Grades follow MWU standards: Quiz (20%), Mid Exam (25%), Assignment (15%), Attendance (10%), Final Exam (30%).

What should I do if I can't submit an assignment?

Contact your lecturer directly through the messaging system or call +251919734417 for urgent issues.

How do I check my attendance record?

Go to the "Attendance" tab in your student portal to view your complete attendance history.
