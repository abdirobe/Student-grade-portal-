Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Change Password" in your dashboard or contact IT support.

Where can I find my course materials?

Go to the "Learning Materials" tab in your student portal.

How are final grades calculated?

Grades are calculated based on: Quiz (20%), Mid Exam (25%), Assignment (15%), Attendance (10%), Final Exam (30%).

What should I do if I can't submit an assignment?

Contact your lecturer directly through the messaging system.
